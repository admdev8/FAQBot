Passenger FAQs:

Q: Is a passenger a person who travels in a vehicle but bears little or no responsibility for the tasks required for that vehicle to arrive at its destination or otherwise operate the vehicle? ¶

A: Yes.

Q: Are passengers people who ride on buses? ¶

A: Yes, and passenger trains, airliners, ships, ferryboats, and other methods of transportation.
